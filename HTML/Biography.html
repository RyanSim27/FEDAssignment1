YOASOBI


Biography:

Yoru ni Kakeru

2019

This was YOASOBI's first song and is still their most played song on spotify. Altough it talks about dark tones, it is an enjoyable song and very catchy to this day.

Kaibutsu (Monster)

2021

This song was made for the anime "Beastars" and was nominated and won the "Best Artist Song Award" for the Anisong Awards in 2021.

Shukufuku

2022

This is the opening for the anime "Mobile Suit Gundam: Witch from Mercury". This song was widely well recieved and was nominated for the "Best Work Award" for the Anisong Awards in 2022.

Idol

2023

This song is the opening for the anime "Oshi no Ko" and was very well recieved by fans. It's music video is their most viewed video on youtube and has won many awards, such as "Song of the Year" by MTV Video Music Awards Japan.

Biri-Biri

2023

Biri-Biri is one of their newest songs and is a collab with the widely known Nintendo game Pokemon. At the time of editing it has been released for 12 days and has 5.7 million views on youtube.
