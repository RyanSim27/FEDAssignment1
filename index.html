About Us:


AYASE: Composer

Ayase was born on April 4, 1994 and has been active in the music industry since 2012. Before he was part of the duo YOASOBI, he was composing with Vocaloid software such as Hatsune Miku. However since 2019 he has been with his fellow duo member Ikura and has since released a bunch of amazing songs in the duo YOASOBI.




IKURA: Vocalist

Real name Lilas Ikuta, stage name Ikura. Born September 25 2000, she started her music career in 2015 but she is now known as the vocalist of YOASOBI. She was always interested in the musical arts, learning the piano during elementry school and playing the guitar during the sixth grade. However, she has grown a lot since those early days and is now the voice of songs such as "IDOL" and "Kaibutsu" from YOASOBI.


TOGETHER: YOASOBI

Yoasobi, a Japanese superduo established in 2019 by Sony Music Entertainment Japan, comprises Vocaloid producer Ayase and singer-songwriter Ikura. Operating under the theme "novel into music," the duo initially created songs inspired by short stories on Monogatary.com, a creative writing platform. Their debut single, "Yoru ni Kakeru," gained immense popularity on social media, dominating the Billboard Japan Hot 100 for six non-consecutive weeks and achieving diamond certification from the Recording Industry Association of Japan for streaming.
